What we are about

ECPortfolios is created during the summer of 2016 when both Eric Cai and Connie Tang attened VrCamp sponsored by Oculus in the Facebook campus. Both Eric and Connie are highschoolers that currently attends Arroyo High School located in San Lorenzo, California. Together, we created an awarding winning virtual reality game utilizing the prototype touch controllers made by Oculus. In addition, we also created a 360 video tour of the Facebook campus using the Richo Theta S.

Eric Cai

Highschooler

Eric Cai is currently a rising senior at Arroyo High School. For the summer of 2016, he attended VrCamp at Facebook Headquarters sponsored by Oculus and TECHSTART. He and his parter, Connie Tang, created many VrGames, 360 videos, and developed websites. Outside of school and camp, Eric is an avid gamer, technology enthusiast, and an adventurer. At the young age of 11 Eric has built his own computer complete with an elaborate multi-monitor set-up, messed with codes on programs and, created video game servers.
